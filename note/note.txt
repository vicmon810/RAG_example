The opportunity - Te tūranga


As a Senior AI Engineer, you will partner closely with an AI Product Owner who leads discovery and prioritisation. Your role is to design, implement, integrate and operate AI solutions, choosing the right approach for each use case. You will help reduce time-to-value for AI initiatives through reuse, standardisation, and sound engineering practices,


You will be responsible for ensuring AI solutions are secure, scalable, reliable and maintainable. This is a senior, hands-on engineering role focused on delivering AI solutions that work in practice.


Your focus will be on

Design, deliver, and operate AI-enabled solutions that support content, commercial, and operational workflows across TVNZ, selecting the most appropriate approach for each use case (custom build, managed service, or off-the-shelf tool)

Implement and integrate generative AI and automation services (including AWS-native capabilities and approved third-party tools) into existing TVNZ systems in a secure and scalable way


This is an 18 Month Fixed term contract

Strong technical background in software engineering.

8+ years of experience in software engineering and/or applied AI roles.
